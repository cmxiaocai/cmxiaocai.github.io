>查找每分钟最大请求数据

~~~bash
grep 'dededynamicapi_v2.php' manager.hiapk.com-aceess.log | grep '08/Sep/2015:[0-9][0-9]:.*' | awk '{print $4}' | awk -F":" '{print $2":"$3}' | uniq -c | sort -nr | head -n 10
~~~
